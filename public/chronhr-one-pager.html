What Chronhr does

Chronhr turns fragmented alerts into investigation-ready cases and SAR-ready reports.

Instead of forcing analysts to chase data across disconnected systems, Chronhr automatically builds the case context, surfaces entity relationships, guides the review, and prepares structured investigation reports without replacing your existing AML stack.

Investigation-ready case files

At alert open, Chronhr auto-assembles transactions, KYC, sanctions, prior alerts, counterparties, and related parties into a single case workspace.

Entity links surfaced instantly

Chronhr maps customers, accounts, businesses, and counterparties so investigators see the full network behind each alert immediately.

Standardized review, stronger defensibility

Every analyst follows the same structured path, reducing variability, missed steps, and weak documentation.

SAR-ready report generation

When escalation is needed, Chronhr produces a structured SAR-ready draft with facts, transaction context, entity links, red flags, and analyst rationale.

Faster judgment, human-controlled decisions

Chronhr removes manual prep so analysts decide faster while retaining full control of judgment, escalation, and SAR filing.
